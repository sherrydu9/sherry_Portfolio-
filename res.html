Rebranding a Restaurant for Lao
Rebranding / Visual / UI

OVERVIEW

The goal of this brand redesign project is to create a more elegant look and feel to match the vibe of this restaurant. The end result includes a logo redesign, an interactive menu on tablet screen, a new website, print and online ad units, business cards, and a handful of marketing collateral.

BACKGROUND

LaoTable is a Thai style with bar restaurant located downtown San Francisco. Sherry Du is the designer who did the rebranding of the restaurant. She has to do a lot of research of the restaurant background and look at it’s main target audience.

BRANDING

TARGET AUDIENCE

Young professionals want the best of both worlds — they want to enjoy professional success while maintaining a healthy work and life balance. After they put in their hours at work, they want a place to relax and connect with like-minded individuals.
