Rebranding of Teance Fine House
Rebranding / Visual / UI

OVERVIEW

1. Redesigned the company brand: refined the color palette, revamp product packaging, print, and interior design.

2. Facilitated marketing initiatives: created video advertisements, presentations, and merchandising.

3. Web UI/UX improvements. Updated website layout and media as part of rebranding. Managed Google Analytics.

BACKGROUND

Teance began nearly two decades ago as a passion project amongst friends in Berkeley, California, with the goal of providing a context for customers to experience the finest teas in the world. The company wanted to create a space to bridge the gap between the ancient and long-refined tea culture of the East and the tastes of the West, through traditional tea service, education, and storytelling.
